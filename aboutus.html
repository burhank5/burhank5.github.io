About Fayz-e-Husayni Trust

Idara Fayz-e-Husayni was established by the 48th Dai-al-Ajal Syedna Abdulhusain Husamuddin R.A. in Karachi on 1st Zilhaj 1305 Hijri (1st August 1888). Fayz-e-Husayni was founded with the aim of helping pilgrims Hujjaj & Zaereen Kiram in their Haj and Ziyarat travels. Since then with the grace of Duat Mutlaqeen R.A. and the benedictions of 53rd Dai-al-Mutlaq His Holiness Dr Syedna Mufaddal Saifuddin TUS, Fayz-e-Husayni has been assisting Pilgrims in India and abroad in their travels.

The Trust looks after requirements such as orientation, ticketing, visas, accommodation, transportation & guidance. 52nd Dai-al-Mutlaq Syedna Mohammed Burhanuddin R.A. graced Fayz-e-Husayni Trust, Bombay office 3 times with the sharaf of his Ziyafat (visits) and instilled in us the devotion of serving Hujjaj and Imam Husain A.S. Zaereen Kiraam.
