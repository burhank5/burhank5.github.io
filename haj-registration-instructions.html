Important Instructions Regarding Hajj 1446h

Applicants are required to contact their nearest coordinator for registration of Hajj 1446.    

A. Departure City Update    
B. Rida Size Update    

A few pointers to be strictly followed:-

Accommodation will be allocated by Sigatul Haj, there will be No Sector Change, and No accommodation category request will be accepted by FH Mumbai office.

Registration on Fayz-E-Husayni website should be done by the passengers individually, only after reading the Instructions carefully by them.

Amount to be deposited will be divided as follows:-

Payment Stage Amount Deadline
1st Payment (Booking) ₹2,00,000 (per pax) At the time of booking
2nd Payment (Near Haram) ₹3,00,000 By 10th Ramadan
2nd Payment (Mohammedi Makan) ₹2,50,000 By 10th Ramadan
2nd Payment (Azizyah) ₹2,00,000 By 10th Ramadan
3rd Payment ₹50,000 (per pax) By 10th Shawwal
Balance (if any) Remaining amount 15 days before departure

Any correspondence regarding hajj 1446 to be sent to the following email id only - haj1446@fayzehusayni.com

Valid Passport should not be changed once bookings done.

Nationals of Bahrain and Oman cannot apply for Hajj visa from India.

Kindly confirm the passport expiry date before booking; it has to have a validity of 6 months, from the date of departure.

Any Foreign National bookings to be undertaken ONLY after approval from Fayz Office and it should be supported with their OCI or PIO, having a lifelong Indian Visa.

Vaccination related important instructions will be given later.
