    Airport Instructions
    • Sagla Zaireen Kiraam potani flight na 4 kallaq pehle Airport pochi jaay.
    • Airport par Zaireen Kiraam ne Fayze Husayni Mumbai taraf si snacks box aapwa ma aawse.
    • Luggage check in karawana waqat ma aa cheezo nu khayal raakhe:
      • A. Laptop, mobile, power bank ni missal electronic items hand luggage ma raakhe.
      • B. Cash, valuables ane essential medicines ne hand luggage ma raakhe.
      • C. Ek jodi kapra hand luggage ma raakhe, jo kai amar bane ane luggage aawa ma delay thai to ye kaam laagi sake.
      • D. Iron, nail cutter, scissors, liquid 100 ml na upar ane toiletries check in luggage ma raakhe.
    • Agar airport par wheelchair assistance ni zarurat hoi to zaireen kiraam pehla si potana coordinator ne khabar kari de taake airlines ne khabar kari sakai. Wheelchair assistance waste alag charges aapwana rehse.
    Note: Cairo airport ane Jedda airport par peewanu paani available nai hotu tesi khali paani ni bottle saathe raakhe ane Bombay si flight board karwa pehle paani bhari le.
    Medicine & Health Instructions
    • Je roz lewa ni dawa hoi ye jitna din ni safar che ehna hisaabe pehla si saathe pack kari le, Karbala, Najaf ya Makkah ma ye specific dawa nabhi mile.
    • Loose motion, shardi, bukhar, body pain, acidity, headache ane ye missal ni bimari waste dawa saathe rakhwu.
    • Influenza vaccine lewu advisable che.
    • Electrol powder ane bhi saathe raakhi sakai che hydration waste.
    Accommodation Instructions
    1. Karbala & Najaf (Faiz Accommodation)
    • Karbala ne Najaf bewe jagah Faiz ma 4 jan na rooms che. Tesi jo couple hase ya 3 jan hase to mardo bairo ne alag alag rehwa nu nizam che.
    • Mehramdar na hoi group ma to mardo bairo alag alag rooms ma rahe.
    • Najaf ma Faiz ni 3 building che (Burhani, Saifee & Badri). Ye teeme ma si jem availability hase emm allocate karwa ma aawse.
    2. Karbala & Najaf (Hotel Accommodation)
    • Hotel ma 2 jan ane 3 jan na rooms available hoi che.
    • Zaireen aa cheez ni nondh le ke Karbala ane Najaf ma hotels normal category ni che.
    3. Makkah & Madina (Accommodation)
    • Makkah ane Madina ma Faize Hashemi zaireen kiraam waste utara nu arrangements kare che.
    • Jem availability hase ye mutabiq ya to Faiz ma ya hotel ma accommodate karwa ma aawse.
